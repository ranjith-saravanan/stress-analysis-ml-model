# Research Paper Format Prompt

Generate Methods and Results sections suitable for an academic paper. Include:
- Study design and sample size justification.
- Data generation or collection details and preprocessing steps.
- Statistical tests and modelling approach (with equations and assumptions).
- Results tables: descriptive stats, correlation matrix, regression coefficients, cluster summaries.
- Figures: publication-ready plots (PNG/SVG), labeled and captioned.

Also provide code to generate the tables and figures from pandas DataFrames so they can be inserted into manuscripts.
